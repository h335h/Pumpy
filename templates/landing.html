Pumpy

Semantic news aggregator for scientific literature

Pumpy collects articles from leading journals, filters them by semantic relevance to your interests, and delivers a personalized digest. Powered by state-of-the-art embeddings and hybrid search.

Get started → Sign in

Why Pumpy?

🧠

Semantic relevance

Uses state-of-the-art embeddings (BAAI/bge-small-en-v1.5) to understand what you care about.

🔍

Hybrid search

Combines BM25 keyword search with semantic similarity for the best of both worlds.

🎯

Personalization

Your likes and dislikes shape your own interest vector — the more you use it, the better it gets.

📚

Diverse results

MMR diversity ensures you see a wide range of relevant articles, not just duplicates.

⚙️

Admin control

Administrators can manage RSS feeds, users, and ranking parameters in a dedicated panel.

🌐

Open source

Fully transparent and extensible — check the code on GitHub.

How it works

1

Collect

Pumpy fetches the latest preprints and articles from RSS feeds of major journals.

2

Filter & rank

Articles are filtered by semantic similarity to your profile, then ranked with hybrid search and MMR.

3

Personalize

Your feedback (likes/dislikes) fine‑tunes your personal interest vector. The system adapts.

4

Enjoy

Every day you get a fresh digest of the most relevant and diverse papers in your field.
